Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

Combine Analysis collects test and workout data that you manually enter, including 40 yard dash times, 10 yard splits, vertical jump, broad jump, agility drill times, bench press reps, drill entries, stride entries, and training sessions. The app optionally requests location access to fetch weather conditions (temperature, wind speed, humidity) for your training sessions. If granted, your approximate location coordinates are sent to a weather API service to retrieve conditions for your session. Location is only accessed when you create a new session and can be skipped by entering weather conditions manually. No HealthKit data, camera, microphone, or other device sensor data is accessed.

Data Storage

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No workout data is sent to external servers or cloud services. When location-based weather fetch is used, your approximate coordinates are sent to a weather API to retrieve conditions — this data is used only for the weather request and is not stored or shared.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All your workout data remains private on your device. The Share Performance feature generates an image that you choose to share — no data is transmitted automatically.

Location Access

Combine Analysis may request location access (NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription) to automatically fetch weather conditions for your training sessions. Location access is optional — you can decline and enter weather conditions manually. When used, your approximate location is sent to a weather service to retrieve temperature, wind, and humidity data. Your location is not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

No HealthKit or Sensor Access

Combine Analysis does not access HealthKit, camera, microphone, accelerometer, or any other device sensors. All combine test results and workout data are manually entered by you.

Notifications

If you enable workout reminders, Combine Analysis uses local notifications on your device to remind you to log your sessions. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent from any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This permanently removes all workouts, profile information, and settings from your device.

Subscription and Trial

Combine Analysis Pro offers a 14-day free trial. If you do not subscribe after the trial, you will continue to have access to free features including manual test entry, analysis, and workout history. Pro features such as advanced charts, trends, and export may be locked. Your workout data is never deleted when the trial expires. Subscribing to Combine Analysis Pro gives you access to advanced features but does not change our data collection or privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Combine Analysis is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. The app does not collect personal data from any user — all data is self-entered workout information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@accel-8.com.
