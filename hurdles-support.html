Hurdles Performance Analysis for iOS.

Getting Started

Logging a Session

Tap 'New Session' to create a hurdle workout. Select your workout type, surface, and timing method. Add hurdle reps by entering distance, hurdle count, time, and rest. Save when done.

Understanding Trends

The Trends tab shows per-distance analysis including best times, average times, speed scores, and trend direction. Select a specific distance or view all distances at once.

Hurdle Specifications

Hurdle Distances

Hurdles Coach supports 60mH (5 hurdles), 100mH (10 hurdles), 110mH (10 hurdles), and 400mH (10 hurdles). You can also log custom distances with manual hurdle counts.

Speed Scores

Speed scores are calculated based on your time relative to expected elite times for each distance, adjusted for surface conditions. Scores range from 0-100.

Subscription

Pro unlocks advanced analytics, race predictions, split analysis, performance sharing, and more. Enjoy a 14-day free trial when you first install the app.
