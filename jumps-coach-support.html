Getting Started

Logging a Session

Tap 'New Session' to create a jump workout. Select your event, surface, and timing method. Add jump reps by entering distance, jump type, time, and rest. Save when done.

Understanding Trends

The Trends tab shows per-event analysis including best distances, average distances, speed scores, and trend direction. Select a specific event or view all events at once.

Jump Events

Supported Events

Jumps Coach supports Long Jump and Triple Jump. You can log custom jump sessions with manual distance and rep counts.

Speed Scores

Speed scores are calculated based on your time relative to expected elite times for each event, adjusted for surface conditions. Scores range from 0-100.
