### 3. App purpose and target audience
SC40 Analysis is a sprint-performance tracking and analysis tool for athletes and coaches who want to log, review, and improve short-distance sprint times and training data.

### 4. How to access the main features
No account is required. Complete onboarding, log a sprint session, view personal bests and analysis cards, and access trends. To view the paywall, tap the **SC40 Pro Active** row in the More tab or open a Pro-gated feature.

### 5. External services and frameworks
- **StoreKit** — for auto-renewable subscriptions and purchase restoration.
- **SwiftData** — local persistence for sessions, profile, and analysis results.
- **UserNotifications** — used only if the user opts in, for workout and trial reminders.
- No third-party authentication, AI, advertising, or external payment processors.

### 6. Regional differences
The app functions identically across all regions. Pricing is localised by the App Store.

### 7. Regulated content or protected material
None. All content and analysis logic are built in-house.
