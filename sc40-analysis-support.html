Getting Started

Welcome to SC40 Analysis

SC40 Analysis is your professional sprint training and analysis companion. Log your workouts manually, track your progress with detailed analytics, and learn from expert training resources.

Quick Start Guide

  • 1. Use the Workout tab to log your drills, strides, and sprint times
  • 2. Or use Manual Entry from the More tab for single sprint analysis
  • 3. View your progress in History and Analysis tabs
  • 4. Explore 40 Yard Smart for drills, technique, and training tips

What You'll Need

  • A stopwatch or timing system for your sprints
  • Open space (40+ yards, flat surface)
  • Proper footwear (sprint spikes or cleats recommended)
  • 30-45 minutes per session

Training Frequency

We recommend 2-4 sessions per week. More isn't always better — quality and recovery are crucial for speed development.

Workouts

Manual Workout Entry

Use the Workout tab to log your training sessions in real time. Add drills, strides, and sprints as you complete them, then save the full session to your history.

Manual Sprint Entry

Use Manual Entry from the More tab to log a single sprint with detailed analysis. Enter your time, distance, and conditions to get instant performance insights.

Workout Structure

  • Warm-up: Dynamic stretching and activation
  • Drills: Technical work (A-skips, B-skips, etc.)
  • Strides: Build-up sprints at 70-80%
  • Main Sprints: Full effort 40-yard dashes
  • Cool-down: Light jogging and static stretching

40 Yard Smart

Access comprehensive training resources covering drills, technique, and recovery. Learn proper form with detailed instructions, coaching cues, and common mistakes to avoid.

Features

  • Manual Workout Entry — Log drills, strides, and sprints in real time. Track every rep and build a complete training history.
  • Sprint Analysis — Enter a single sprint time and get instant analysis with performance insights and personal best tracking.
  • Progress Analytics — Track improvement over time with detailed charts, trends, and statistics.
  • Workout History — Review all past workouts with times, distances, drill entries, and performance metrics.
  • Share Performance — Generate professional reports to share with coaches, recruiters, or on social media.
  • 40 Yard Smart — Comprehensive training resources covering drills, technique, and recovery with expert coaching cues.

Troubleshooting

Workout Not Saving

  • Make sure you've added at least one rep or drill before saving
  • Check that the session has a name and date
  • Try force quitting and reopening the app
  • Contact support if data loss persists

Sprints Not Appearing in History

  • Pull down to refresh the History view
  • Check that you pressed Save Workout after your session
  • Manual Entry sprints are also saved as workout sessions
  • Try restarting the app to refresh the database

Analysis Shows No Data

  • Make sure you have saved workouts in your History
  • Check the time range filter in Analysis view
  • Manual Entry sprints appear in Analysis after saving
  • Try switching between time ranges (Week/Month/All)

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force quit and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Go to Settings > Reset All Data to clear corrupted data
  • Contact support at support@accel-8.com if issues persist

FAQ

General

What is SC40 Analysis?

SC40 Analysis is a professional sprint training and analysis app designed to help athletes improve their 40-yard dash time through manual workout logging, detailed performance analytics, and expert training resources.

Do I need any special equipment?

You need an iPhone and a way to time your sprints (stopwatch, timing gates, or coach). Sprint spikes, a track, or open field are recommended but not required.

Is SC40 Analysis suitable for beginners?

Yes! SC40 Analysis works for all experience levels. The 40 Yard Smart section provides drills and technique guidance for beginner through elite athletes.

How is SC40 Analysis different from Sprint Coach 40?

SC40 Analysis focuses on manual workout entry and analysis without GPS tracking. You log your times manually and get detailed analytics and educational content. Sprint Coach 40 includes GPS-tracked workouts and Apple Watch integration.

Workout Entry

How do I log a workout?

Go to the Workout tab, add your drills, strides, and sprints using the Add Interval button. Enter the distance and time for each rep, then press Save Workout to store the session.

What is Manual Entry?

Manual Entry (from the More tab) lets you log a single sprint with detailed analysis. Enter your time, distance, and conditions, and the app analyzes your performance and saves it to your history.

Can I edit a workout after saving?

Currently, saved workouts cannot be edited. If you need to correct a mistake, you can delete the workout from History and re-enter it.

What drills and strides can I log?

You can log any drill type and distance. Common drills include A-skips, B-skips, high knees, and butt kicks. Strides are typically 20-40 yard build-up sprints at 70-80% effort.

How do I know if I'm improving?

Track your progress in the Analysis tab. SC40 Analysis shows your personal best, average times, improvement trends, and detailed analytics for each workout.

Technical

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Reset All Data. WARNING: This permanently deletes all your workouts, progress, and profile data.

Can I export my workout data?

Yes! Use the Share Performance feature to generate detailed reports with your workout history and athlete profile. You can share via text, email, or save as PDF.

What should I do if the app crashes?

Try restarting the app and your device. If issues persist, go to Settings → Reset All Data to clear corrupted data. Contact support at support@accel-8.com with details about when the crash occurred.

Is my data backed up?

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We recommend using the Share Performance feature to export important data periodically.

Can I use SC40 Analysis offline?

Yes! SC40 Analysis works fully offline. All workout entry, analysis, and history features work without an internet connection.
