## App Description (4000 chars max)

Strength Coach Analysis is your dedicated strength-training log and analysis app. Built for athletes and recreational lifters who want to track sets, reps, weight and RPE in one structured place.

Just tap New Session, choose your exercises, enter your sets and the app calculates total volume and estimated one-rep max for each lift.

FEATURES

• Simple Workout Logging
Choose from a built-in exercise library or create custom exercises. Record sets, reps, weight and RPE for every movement. Add session notes and tags so you know exactly how each workout felt.

• Automatic Analysis
Volume and estimated 1RM are calculated automatically as you log. See per-exercise progress without manual spreadsheets.

• Workout History
Browse every session you've saved. Tap any session to see the full breakdown of exercises, sets, reps, weight and total volume.

• Trends & Analytics (Pro)
Unlock per-exercise trend charts, estimated 1RM tracking, volume and RPE analysis, and shareable performance reports. Try Pro free for 14 days.

• Apple Health
Optionally save strength workouts to Apple Health with one tap.

• Workout Reminders
Set optional local reminders so you never miss a training day.

WHY STRENGTH COACH ANALYSIS?

• Your workout data stays on your device — no cloud, no tracking, no third-party sharing.
• Works offline for core workout logging.
• No account, login or subscription required for the workout log.
• 14-day free trial — try every Pro feature before subscribing.

## App Review Information

### Review Notes
Strength Coach Analysis is a manual strength-training log. It does not use HealthKit without permission, camera, microphone or wearable sensors. All sets, reps and weights are manually entered. The optional Pro subscription unlocks trend charts and shareable reports. There is no account, login or user-generated content. App Tracking Transparency is not requested.

### Demo Instructions
1. Launch the app and complete onboarding (name, weight unit, optional athlete profile, safety warning, notification permission).
2. Tap New Session, choose an exercise, and add one or more sets with reps, weight and RPE.
3. Tap Save Session and go to History to view the saved session.
4. Go to Trends/Analytics to view per-exercise charts (available during the 14-day Pro trial).
5. Tap the Pro banner or Upgrade in the More tab to view the paywall.
6. In More → Help & Info and Settings, verify Privacy Policy, Terms and Support links are accessible.
