Strength Coach Analysis — Support

Strength Training Performance Tracker for iOS.

Getting Started

Getting Started

Tap 'New Session' to log a strength training workout. Enter the session name, date, warm-up and warm-down minutes, then add exercises with sets, reps, weight, and RPE for each set you complete.

Exercises

Choose from the built-in exercise library or create custom exercises. For each exercise, log multiple sets with weight, reps, and RPE (rate of perceived exertion). The app calculates total volume and estimated 1RM (one-rep max) using the Epley formula.

Session Templates

Create reusable workout templates to quickly start sessions with your typical exercises pre-loaded. Templates save time when logging recurring workouts.

Analysis & Tracking

Volume & 1RM Tracking

Strength Coach Analysis automatically calculates total session volume (sets × reps × weight) and estimated 1RM for each exercise. Track how your strength progresses over time.

Per-Exercise Trends (Pro)

Strength Coach Pro unlocks per-exercise trend analysis, showing how your volume, 1RM, and RPE change over time for each exercise.

Share Performance (Pro)

Export and share your training reports via the iOS system share sheet. Reports include session summaries, exercise breakdowns, and progress metrics.

Edit Profile

Update your name, age, body weight, height, sport, position, and weight unit preference anytime from the More tab → Edit Profile. All fields entered during onboarding are editable.

Apple Health Integration

HealthKit

Strength Coach Analysis can save your strength workouts to Apple Health as traditional strength training workouts. The app also reads body mass, biological sex, and date of birth from HealthKit to personalise your reports. HealthKit access is optional — you can decline and use the app fully without it.

Data & Privacy

Data & Privacy

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. No personal data or workout results are sent to any server. You can delete all data at any time from Settings → Reset All Data.
