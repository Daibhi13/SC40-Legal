## App Description (4000 chars max)

Sprint Coach is your professional sprint training companion for 60m, 100m, 200m, and 400m — log your training sessions and races, track your progress, and get instant performance analysis.

Built for track athletes, sprint coaches, and anyone serious about improving their sprint speed. Just enter your times and get detailed analytics — no GPS tracking, no wearables required.

FEATURES

• Manual Workout Entry
Log full training sessions in real time. Track drills, strides, and sprints with distance and time for each rep. Save sessions with surface type, timing method, and workout notes.

• Instant Sprint Analysis
Enter a sprint and get immediate feedback — speed score, acceleration score, and sprint profile (Elite, Advanced, Intermediate, or Beginner) based on your time and distance.

• Performance Analytics (Pro)
View progress with detailed charts and statistics. Filter by week, month, or all-time. Track personal bests, average times, consistency scores, and improvement trends.

• Advanced Charts & Trends (Pro)
Deep insights with progress trends, RPE analysis, and rest consistency tracking.

• Share Performance (Pro)
Generate professional reports with personal bests, statistics, and session history. Share via email, message, or PDF with coaches and recruiters.

• Export Data (Pro)
Export workout history as CSV or PDF for backup, sharing, or importing into other tools.

• Workout History
Browse all past sessions with full detail — every rep, drill, stride, and analysis result. Filter by workout type, surface, or date range.

• Sprint Training Resources
Training resources covering sprint drills, technique, and recovery. Learn proper form with coaching cues, common mistakes, and drill progressions for all levels.

• Personal Best Tracking
Your personal best is tracked automatically across 60m, 100m, 200m, and 400m. See improvement over time and get notified on new records.

• Workout Reminders
Set custom reminder notifications for your training days so you never miss a session.

WHY SPRINT COACH?

• No GPS tracking — works anywhere, indoors or outdoors
• Your workout data stays on your device — no cloud, no tracking, no third-party sharing
• Optional location for weather — your approximate location can be used to auto-fetch weather conditions for your session. This is optional and can be skipped by entering weather manually.
• Works offline — no internet connection needed for core features
• 14-day free trial — try all Pro features before subscribing

Whether you're training for track season, competing in sprint races, or just want to get faster, Sprint Coach helps you track your progress and train smarter.

## App Review Information

### Review Notes
Sprint Coach is a manual 40-yard dash entry and analysis app. It does NOT use HealthKit, camera, microphone, or any wearable sensors. All sprint times are manually entered by the user. The app optionally requests CoreLocation access to fetch weather conditions (temperature, wind, humidity) for training sessions — this is optional and can be skipped by entering weather manually. Location data is not stored or shared. The app works fully offline for core features.

There is no login or account system. The reviewer can use the app immediately after completing onboarding.

### Demo Instructions
1. Complete onboarding — enter your name, age, weight, height, sport, position, and personal best time.
2. Go to the **New Session** tab — select workout type, enter a distance (e.g., 40yd), enter a time (e.g., 4.72s), and tap "Add Rep". Add 2-3 reps. You can also log drills and strides.
3. Tap **Save Session** — the session saves to History.
4. Go to **History** tab — verify the saved session appears with rep details and analysis.
5. Go to **Trends** tab — verify charts and metrics appear for the saved sessions (Pro feature, available during 14-day trial).
6. Go to **More** tab — explore Share Performance, Export Data, Edit Profile, Settings, and Help & Info.
7. In Settings, verify Support Email, Privacy Policy and Terms of Service are accessible.

### How to Resize Screenshots for Each Slot

If you want to manually upload screenshots for every size (instead of relying on auto-scaling):

1. **Capture once** at the highest resolution (6.9" = 1320 × 2868)
2. **Resize** for each target size using Preview.app or `sips`:
   ```bash
   # Example: resize a 6.9" screenshot to 6.5"
   sips --resampleHeightWidth 2778 1284 screenshot-69.png --out screenshot-65.png

   # Resize to 6.3"
   sips --resampleHeightWidth 2622 1206 screenshot-69.png --out screenshot-63.png

   # Resize to 6.1"
   sips --resampleHeightWidth 2532 1170 screenshot-69.png --out screenshot-61.png

   # Resize to 5.5"
   sips --resampleHeightWidth 2208 1242 screenshot-69.png --out screenshot-55.png

   # Resize to 4.7"
   sips --resampleHeightWidth 1334 750 screenshot-69.png --out screenshot-47.png
   ```
3. **Or just upload 6.9"** and let App Store Connect auto-scale to all other sizes.

> **Important**: Screenshots must be `.png`, `.jpg`, or `.jpeg`. No device frame. Portrait orientation. Min 1, max 10 per device size.
