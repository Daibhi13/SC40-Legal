Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

Strength Coach Analysis collects workout data that you manually enter, including exercises, sets, reps, weights, drill entries, and training sessions. The app optionally requests location access to fetch weather conditions (temperature, wind speed, humidity) for your training sessions. If granted, your approximate location coordinates are sent to a weather API service to retrieve conditions for your session. Location is only accessed when you create a new session and can be skipped by entering weather conditions manually. With your permission, the app can also read body mass, biological sex, and date of birth from Apple Health to help prefill your profile, and you may choose to share completed strength workouts to Apple Health.

Data Storage

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Workout templates are stored locally in your device's settings (UserDefaults). No workout data is sent to external servers or cloud services. When location-based weather fetch is used, your approximate coordinates are sent to a weather API to retrieve conditions — this data is used only for the weather request and is not stored or shared. Workouts you choose to save to Apple Health are stored in the Health app and may sync through iCloud if you have HealthKit iCloud sync enabled; we do not store or process that data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All your workout data remains private on your device. The Share Performance feature generates an image that you choose to share — no data is transmitted automatically.

Location Access

Strength Coach Analysis may request location access (NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription) to automatically fetch weather conditions for your training sessions. Location access is optional — you can decline and enter weather conditions manually. When used, your approximate location is sent to a weather service to retrieve temperature, wind, and humidity data. Your location is not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

HealthKit Access

With your permission, Strength Coach Analysis can read body mass, biological sex, and date of birth from Apple Health to help prefill your athlete profile. You can also choose to share completed strength workouts to Apple Health. HealthKit integration is optional and can be enabled or disabled in your device settings. Camera, microphone, and other device sensors are not accessed.

Notifications

If you enable workout reminders, Strength Coach Analysis uses local notifications on your device to remind you to log your workouts. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent from any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your local app data at any time by going to Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This permanently removes all workouts, profile information, templates, and settings from your device. Data you have saved to Apple Health is stored in the Health app and is not deleted by this action.

Subscription and Trial

Strength Coach Analysis Pro is £1.99/month (or local equivalent) with a 14-day free trial. If you do not subscribe after the trial, you will continue to have access to free features including manual workout entry, analysis, and workout history. Pro features such as per-exercise progress charts, advanced analytics, Apple Health sharing, workout templates, and export will be locked. Your workout data is never deleted when the trial expires. Subscribing does not change our data collection or privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Strength Coach Analysis is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. The app does not collect personal data from any user beyond self-entered workout information and optional HealthKit data you choose to share.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@accel-8.com.
