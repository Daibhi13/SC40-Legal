Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

Strength Coach Analysis collects workout data that you manually enter, including exercise names, sets, reps, weight, RPE (rate of perceived exertion), session details, and athlete profile information (name, age, body weight, height, sport, position). The app optionally requests HealthKit authorization to save your strength workouts to Apple Health and to read basic profile data (body mass, biological sex, date of birth) to personalise your training reports. No camera, microphone, location, accelerometer, or other device sensor data is accessed.

Data Storage

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No workout data is sent to external servers or cloud services. All data remains private on your device.

HealthKit Access

Strength Coach Analysis may request HealthKit authorization to:

  • Write: Save your strength workout sessions to Apple Health as traditional strength training workouts.
  • Read: Body mass, biological sex, and date of birth to personalise your training reports.
HealthKit access is optional — you can decline and use the app fully without it. HealthKit data is not shared with any third party.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All your workout data remains private on your device. The Share Performance feature generates a report that you choose to share via the iOS system share sheet — no data is transmitted automatically.

Notifications

If you enable trial reminders, Strength Coach Analysis uses local notifications on your device to remind you about your subscription trial status. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent from any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Reset All Data. This permanently removes all sessions, exercises, profile information, and settings from your device.

Subscription and Trial

Strength Coach Pro offers a 14-day free trial, then a monthly auto-renewable subscription. If you do not subscribe after the trial, you will continue to have access to free features including manual session entry and workout history. Pro features such as advanced analytics, estimated 1RM tracking, and performance report sharing may be locked. Your workout data is never deleted when the trial expires. Subscribing to Strength Coach Pro gives you access to advanced features but does not change our data collection or privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Strength Coach Analysis is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. The app does not collect personal data from any user — all data is self-entered workout information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@accel-8.com.
