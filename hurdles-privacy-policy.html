Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data Collection

Hurdles Coach collects workout data that you manually enter, including hurdle times, drill entries, stride data, and training sessions. The app does not access location, camera, microphone, HealthKit, accelerometer, or any other device sensors. All data is entered manually by you.

Data Storage

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No workout data is sent to external servers or cloud services. All data remains private on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All your workout data remains private on your device. The Share Performance feature generates an image or file that you choose to share — no data is transmitted automatically.

No Location or Sensor Access

Hurdles Coach does not request or access location data, HealthKit, camera, microphone, accelerometer, or any other device sensors. All hurdle times and workout data are manually entered by you.

Notifications

If you enable workout reminders, Hurdles Coach uses local notifications on your device to remind you to log your sessions. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent from any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This permanently removes all workouts, profile information, and settings from your device.

Subscription and Trial

Hurdles Coach Pro offers a 14-day free trial. If you do not subscribe after the trial, you will continue to have access to free features including manual workout entry, hurdle analysis, and workout history. Pro features such as advanced charts, trends, and export will be locked. Your workout data is never deleted when the trial expires. Subscribing to Hurdles Coach Pro gives you access to advanced features but does not change our data collection or privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Hurdles Coach is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. The app does not collect personal data from any user — all data is self-entered workout information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@accel-8.com.
